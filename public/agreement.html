Lanevox Client Service Agreement

1. Services

Lanevox Consulting Group ABN 62 553 965 783 ("the Agency") agrees to provide the services selected above to the Client. The specific scope, deliverables and timelines for each service will be outlined in a separate onboarding brief following this agreement.

2. Payment Terms

Payment is processed securely via Stripe. A payment link will be provided to the Client separately upon signing this agreement.

Monthly retainer fees are due on the first business day of each calendar month. Upfront setup fees are due prior to commencement of work. All prices are in Australian Dollars (AUD) and are inclusive of GST where applicable.

Late payments beyond 7 days may result in a pause of services until outstanding amounts are settled.

3. No Lock-In Contracts

The Client may cancel ongoing monthly services at any time by providing a minimum of 14 days' written notice to hello@lanevox.com.au. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Upfront setup fees are non-refundable once work has commenced.

4. Intellectual Property

Upon full payment of all outstanding invoices, ownership of deliverables including website files, ad creative, and written copy transfers to the Client. The Agency retains the right to showcase work in its portfolio unless the Client requests otherwise in writing.

Any third-party assets (stock imagery, licensed fonts, plugins) used in the work remain subject to their respective licences.

5. Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to:

  • Provide accurate business information, branding assets, and feedback within agreed timeframes.
  • Designate a single point of contact for approvals and communication.
  • Respond to revision requests and approval prompts within 5 business days to avoid delays.
  • Not resell or sublicence the Agency's work to third parties without prior written consent.

6. Limitation of Liability

The Agency will perform all services with reasonable skill and care. However, the Agency does not guarantee specific outcomes such as search engine rankings, ad performance metrics, or social media growth, as these are influenced by factors outside the Agency's control.

The Agency's total liability for any claim shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client in the three calendar months preceding the claim.

7. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep each other's confidential business information private and not to disclose it to third parties without written consent. This obligation continues for two years following termination of the agreement.

8. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of Queensland, Australia. Any disputes will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Queensland.
